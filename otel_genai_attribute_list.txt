Common (most spans)

gen_ai.operation.name — chat | generate_content | text_completion | embeddings | retrieval | execute_tool | create_agent | invoke_agent | invoke_workflow
gen_ai.provider.name — e.g. gcp.gen_ai, gcp.vertex_ai, openai, anthropic, aws.bedrock
gen_ai.conversation.id — session correlation (ties an attack conversation together)
gen_ai.agent.id, gen_ai.agent.name, gen_ai.agent.description
gen_ai.data_source.id — RAG/grounding source
error.type — on failure
server.address, server.port

Response: gen_ai.response.id, gen_ai.response.model, gen_ai.response.finish_reasons, gen_ai.usage.input_tokens, gen_ai.usage.output_tokens

Embeddings

gen_ai.request.model, gen_ai.request.encoding_formats, gen_ai.embeddings.dimension.count, gen_ai.usage.input_tokens
